My Favorite Sports

A Legacy of Greatness, Where Every Play Shapes History

My Favorite Sport

One of my favorite sports growing up was basketball, me and my brothers played everyday from sun up to sun down and even tried playing at night whenever we could. It was always me and my brother Juelz who would go play at parks for money so we could buy snacks. I loved basketball so much i would ditch all my freshman classes just to go to P.E and play basketball all day, then go home and play till sun down. I learned from a highschool coach who lived two houses down and he encouraged me to put 100% into anything I do. Thank you Coach B.

My Favorite Teams/Players

NBA: Chicago Bulls, Milwaukee Bucks. Players: Derrick Rose, Jimmy Butler, Liangelo Ball, Michael Jordan, Ben Wallace, and Zach Lavine.

My Favorite Sport

My Father used to make me and my siblings box in the backyard whenever we had conflict. We grew up around boxing and although at first I didn't care for it, around middle school I started to fall in love with boxing. It may seem barbaric to others but for me, boxing was art. A way to express myself without being judged and non-stop learning through out my journey. There is a certain science to boxing that when slowed down truly shows the skills and IQ of each fighter. I think it's a powerful way to release emotions as well, also building up resillience to various aspects in life. I want to thank My father for showing me how to not only box, but to be able to learn and improve through my own analysis and to think for myself.

My Favorite Fighters

Roberto Duran, Dimitry Bivol, Miguel Cotto, Vasily Lomachenko, Terrence Crawford, Reddick Bowe.
